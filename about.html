Mai See Xiong studies at the University of Wisconsin Eau-Claire majoring English, Creative Writing emphasis, minoring in Topical Critical Hmong Studies.

She is also working on two certificates: Interdisciplinary Linguistics and Women, Gender, and Sexualities Studies.

Mai See aims to change the world little by little with her literature as well as share her own perspective being a Hmong woman, the youngest daughter and the first in her family to graduate from a 4-year university.
