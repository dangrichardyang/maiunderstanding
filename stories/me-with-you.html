Me With You

August 4, 2014 at 7:08 PM

Today I'm thinking about you.

It is always of you.

No one else enters my head,

but you come so easy.

When I don't see you, I'm sad.

I want you to be with me.

Your smiles are everything.

I don't need anything like friends or family.

Just you and me every single day.

I don't even care about what they say.

As long as we're happy, nothing matters.

Even if the world were to shatter,

with you by my side, I have no fears.

All the thoughts that are processed, who cares.

Our romance is real.

Our love is the truth.

Only we know how this feels.

It's fine with just us two.
