The Tears I Shed Became Courage

My tears that roughly drop,

Will there be someone to hold me?

As I stand here looking at the sky,

I really am nothing but a tree.


'No one will care' I thought,

'I really won't be known.'

I exist through many years,

But I won't really be shown.


Many seasons past by,

Not one person notice.

Many times I have thought,

If only i was born as a lotus.


My bitter tears fall,

Just then a girl appeared.

She reached out to me and said,

'This tree is so weird.'


Once more i am filled,

This courage in me won't stop.

Happiness overcame me,

Until the day that I drop.
